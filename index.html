When you use the Discord.Rocks API, we may collect the following types of information:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • To Comply with Laws

4. Data Security

We employ a variety of security measures to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your personal data. These include physical, technical, and administrative measures.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and for other essential purposes such as complying with our legal obligations and resolving disputes.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding the personal data we hold about you. These may include the right to access, correct, delete, or transfer your data, or restrict its processing.
